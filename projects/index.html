Curriculum Vitae

Selected Projects

Enzyme Design and Computational Enzymology

  • ROADMAP for catalytic activity landscape modeling and adaptive enzyme engineering. Built a unified AI framework to model catalytic activity across sequence, substrate, pH, and temperature in sparse-data settings, with strong benchmarking and low-budget wet-lab validation.
  • EnzyControl for substrate-aware enzyme backbone generation. Helped shape a benchmark and generation framework for function- and substrate-aware enzyme design, improving designability and catalytic efficiency.
  • Protein-sequence-aware enzymatic reaction prediction. Led model conception and data integration for predicting enzyme reaction outcomes directly from protein sequence and substrate structure.

Protein Conformation and Macromolecular Modeling

  • ProteinConformers. Led development of a large-scale benchmark and resource for protein conformational landscapes with geometry optimization, energetic profiling, and new evaluation metrics.
  • ONION. Developed an environment-aware inverse folding framework spanning proteins, DNA, and RNA in monomers and complexes.
  • DPStab. Co-led modeling work on mutation-induced protein stability change prediction using protein language models, contact-aware attention, and self-distillation.
  • Protein structure pretraining and differentiable energy modeling. Explored all-atom structural encoders, conformational similarity learning, and deep potential modeling for downstream structure tasks.
  • High-throughput protein structure prediction platform. Designed and deployed an industrial prediction workflow with multi-model, multi-parameter, and multi-GPU support for discovery-scale use cases.

Structure-based Molecular Generation

  • DrugRPG. Co-led a representation-aligned and physics-guided diffusion framework for pocket-conditioned 3D molecule generation with stronger physical plausibility and affinity-related metrics.
  • PepScape. Helped shape a pocket-conditioned multi-objective peptide generation framework for binding affinity and developability optimization.
  • Flexible docking under receptor and ligand flexibility. Contributed to a framework for realistic docking and pose prediction under conformational variability.
  • Transferable molecular representation learning. Supported a unified encoding-decoding framework for virtual screening, property prediction, and controllable design.

Multi-omics and Genomics Modeling

  • OUTPOST. First-author leadership on a full-stack software platform for whole-metagenome shotgun analysis and multi-group experimental design.
  • TRIP. Developed a large-scale telomeric repeat identification pipeline and led comparative evolutionary analysis across insect species.
  • Cross-species single-cell analysis of vertebrate pineal gland evolution. Co-first-author work on cross-species integration and biological interpretation.
  • Single-cell characterization of cerebrospinal-fluid tumor cells. Co-first-author work on single-cell transcriptomic analysis for lung-cancer leptomeningeal metastasis.
