Curriculum Vitae

Hello, I’m Yihang Zhou, a scientist working on AI for drug discovery, molecular modeling, generative design, and bioinformatics.

I am currently a Research Fellow at the School of Computing and the Cancer Science Institute, National University of Singapore. My work spans protein conformational landscape modeling, inverse folding, enzyme intelligence, structure-based molecular generation, and biological foundation-model directions.

Over the past several years, I have worked across academia and industry on projects involving protein modeling, drug discovery, single-cell and metagenomic analysis, and scientific software systems. I enjoy building end-to-end research programs, from problem formulation and dataset construction to modeling, evaluation, and publication.

My broader interest is to make computational biology and molecular design more predictive, scalable, and useful in real scientific settings.

Highlights

  • Research Fellow at National University of Singapore
  • 9 years of experience spanning AI, bioinformatics, and molecular science
  • Work across protein design, molecular generation, enzyme modeling, and omics
  • Experience in academic, translational, and industrial drug discovery settings

Selected Research Directions

  • Protein conformation and inverse folding
  • Structure-based molecular and peptide generation
  • Enzyme modeling and adaptive engineering
  • Bioinformatics, genomics, and multi-omics analysis
